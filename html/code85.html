Note

article purpose: Use for self-contained content that makes sense alone — blog posts, news items, comments, product cards.

Independent: If content could be syndicated in RSS or shared alone and still make sense, use <article>.

Structure: Can contain <header>, <footer>, <section>, headings, and other articles nested inside.

article vs section: <article> = standalone piece. <section> = thematic grouping. If unsure, ask "would this make sense in an RSS reader?"

Multiple allowed: Page can have many <article> elements. Each should have its own heading for structure.
